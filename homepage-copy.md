**Tagline:** Your design system, codified

**Tagline:** Specs before code, not after

**Tagline:** A counterpart for every design decision

**Description:** Reviews every UI across typography, color, layout, and overflow. Tests at mobile, tablet, and desktop. Quality control at scale, without designer time.

---

### Why Skills, not MCP?

**Native, not bolted-on**
Your team keeps working with their coding agents and native capabilities. No third-party dependencies, no extra approvals, no MCP prompts cluttering your CLI.

**Optimized for performance**
Skills are tuned for efficient context usage and delegate work to sub-agents that run in parallel. Fast, lean, and cost-effective.

**Updated with the platform**
When coding agents ship new capabilities, we update the skills to take advantage. You stay current without the work.

**No lock-in—to anyone**
The skills format has been adopted by VS Code, OpenAI, and other top agent providers. You're not locked into a specific stack, or to Chordio. Take your skills wherever you go.
