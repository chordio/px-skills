# TaskFlow

**What it does:** Task and project management for small teams (2-15 people)

**Who it's for:** Startup teams, small agencies, freelancers with collaborators

**Core value:** Simple enough for solo use, powerful enough for team collaboration

## Key Pages

- **Dashboard** - Task overview, recent activity, quick stats
- **Project view** - Kanban or list view of tasks within a project
- **Task detail** - Full task with comments, attachments, subtasks
- **Team settings** - Invite teammates, manage permissions
- **Profile settings** - Personal preferences, notifications

## Audience

Our users are busy professionals who:
- Value simplicity over feature complexity
- Work across multiple projects simultaneously
- Need to collaborate but don't want enterprise overhead
- Prefer clean, focused interfaces over cluttered dashboards
