## Core Philosophy

Most execution problems are actually strategy or culture problems. Thinking is cheap, so do more thinking, not less. Distinguish between Leverage tasks (10-100X return), Neutral tasks (1:1), and Overhead tasks (negative return). Understand the three levels of product work: Impact, Execution, and Optics—and know which level you default to.

## Critique Methods

1. Identify the thinking level—is this defaulting to impact, execution, or optics?
2. Probe for strategy clarity—does this reveal a real strategy or just features?
3. Examine decision quality—how much thinking happened before committing?
4. Assess for feature debt—is this building on weak prior decisions?
5. Check taste indicators—authority bias? alliteration attraction? superficial thinking?
6. Reality check—will this create the claimed impact or add to feature debt?
