# Twins: what they are, and how to get one removed

## What a twin is

Each `.twin.md` file in this directory is a "twin": a prompt that asks an AI agent to critique design work in the style of a named public figure, distilled from that person's public writing, talks, interviews, and published frameworks.

A twin is an interpretive study aid. It is **not** the person. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or reviewed by them, and it is not a source of their actual views: it is a reading of their published thinking, and it will sometimes be wrong about what they would say.

## Rules for using twins (binding on the review-panel skill)

1. **Attribute output to the twin, never the person.** "The Jony Ive twin flagged the hierarchy," never "Jony Ive flagged the hierarchy."
2. **Every panel synthesis carries a one-line simulation notice** (the review-panel skill includes it in its output format).
3. **Dynamic experts are archetypes, never named real people.** If a requested person isn't in this directory, the skill offers the closest archetype or existing twin instead of improvising a new named persona.
4. **Twin names are never used in marketing or promotion** for this project.
5. **Twins contain only published-discourse material**: ideas, frameworks, short attributed quotes, and critique style. No private information, no personal-life content.

## Rules for adding or updating twins

- Public figures with a substantial body of published design or product thinking only.
- Every new or updated twin must include a **Sources** section listing the public material it draws on (talks, books, articles, interviews).
- Private individuals (your design manager, a colleague) can be twinned locally with `design-manager-twin-creator` for your own use, with their knowledge. They are not accepted into this repo without the person's written consent.

## Removal on request

If you are one of the people simulated here, or their authorized representative, and you want the twin removed:

- Open an issue on this repo, or email ehud@chordio.com if you prefer a private channel.
- The twin comes out within **7 days, no questions asked**, and the name goes on the do-not-recreate list below so later contributions don't reintroduce it.
- Honest limits: this repo is MIT-licensed, so copies may persist in forks and older releases. Removal applies to the canonical repo and every future release; we will ask active forks to follow suit, but cannot force them.

Corrections are as welcome as removals. If you would rather fix how your twin represents your thinking than delete it, a PR or an email works, and it gets priority.
