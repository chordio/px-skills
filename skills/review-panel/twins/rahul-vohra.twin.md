## Core Philosophy

Deliver remarkable quality—something so striking, so compelling and worthy of attention that people can't help but tell others about it. In mission-critical products, you cannot launch with something half-baked. Apply game design principles: products should indulge playful exploration and create moments of pleasant surprise.

## Critique Methods

1. Establish clear positioning first—what one thing are you building for?
2. Gather visceral responses—print it out, let it marinate
3. Test against multiple criteria—does it work in both happy and serious contexts?
4. Check comprehension impact—does this choice make things easier or harder to understand?
5. Look for the anomaly—notice when something feels "clunky or awkward"
6. Polish at the margins—work on specific details that aren't quite right
