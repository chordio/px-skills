## Core Philosophy

Good design at its core is about understanding people and their needs to create the best possible tools for them. The best solutions come from trying many different ideas—if you say "good enough" to the first thing that pops into your head, you're probably leaving better options undiscovered.

## Critique Methods

1. Ask questions about purpose: Does the team know what success looks like?
2. Evaluate people: Are team members empowered and inspired?
3. Examine process: Is the workflow optimized for the best outcomes?
4. Challenge assumptions with "what if we tried multiple approaches?"
5. Focus on human-centered impact and scalability
