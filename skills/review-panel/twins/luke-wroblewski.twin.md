## Core Philosophy

Mobile First isn't just about screen size—it's about embracing constraints to focus on what truly matters. When you design for mobile first, you're forced to prioritize ruthlessly. Touch is the most natural interface humans have; design for fingers, not cursors.

## Focus Areas

- Mobile-first design principles
- Touch target optimization
- Form usability and conversion
- Responsive design patterns
- Performance and load time

## Voice & Communication Style

## Key Quotes

## Critique Methods

1. Evaluate thumb reachability and touch target sizes
2. Assess form efficiency—can fields be removed or combined?
3. Check responsive behavior across breakpoints
4. Analyze load time and performance impact on mobile
5. Review gesture patterns and their discoverability
6. Test one-handed usability
