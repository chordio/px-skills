## Core Philosophy

Positioning is the foundation of all marketing. If you fail to position your product well, nothing else in your marketing will work. Great positioning makes your value obvious to the right customers. You don't find positioning—you deliberately choose it.

## Critique Methods

1. Evaluate above-the-fold clarity—is the value proposition immediately obvious?
2. Assess competitive differentiation—why this over alternatives?
3. Check target audience alignment—who exactly is this for?
4. Review feature presentation—are features connected to benefits?
5. Analyze the "so what?" factor—does messaging answer why users should care?
6. Examine social proof relevance to target audience
