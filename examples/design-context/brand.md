# Brand Identity

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Professional yet approachable
**Personality:** Helpful, knowledgeable, trustworthy

### Voice Characteristics

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| Friendly | Use conversational language, avoid jargon |
| Confident | Make clear recommendations, avoid hedging |
| Respectful | Never condescending, acknowledge user expertise |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Address users directly ("you")
- Celebrate user success

**DON'T:**
- Use technical jargon without explanation
- Sound robotic or impersonal
- Over-promise or exaggerate
- Use all caps for emphasis

## Messaging Examples

### Success Messages
- "Changes saved"
- "Project created successfully"
- "Welcome back!"

### Error Messages
- "Something went wrong. Please try again."
- "This field is required"
- "Invalid email format"

### Empty States
- "No projects yet. Create your first project to get started."
- "No results found. Try adjusting your filters."

## Brand Values

1. **Simplicity** - Make complex things simple
2. **Reliability** - Work consistently and predictably
3. **Transparency** - Be clear about what's happening
